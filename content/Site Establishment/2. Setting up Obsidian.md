## Create a Vault

![[Screenshot 2025-04-23 at 10.56.35 AM.png]]
I suggest setting up a standalone vault specifically to manage your website and related content.
- The main reasoning behind a dedicated vault is to prevent personal or otherwise private information from being mistakenly pushed to your website.
- With careful curating and attention, this should not happen, but let's stack the deck in our favor and not risk our biscuits (it's a secret family recipe).

If you have vaults on your device, you should be prompted with the options depicted above in the image.
1. Select either *Create a new vault* or *Open a folder as a vault* (whichever suits you best).
	- In practice these two options will produce the exact same result
		- *Create a new vault :* will setup a vault folder in a location you specify.
		- *Open a folder as a vault :* will designate an existing folder you specify as a vault.

>[!note]+ Keep in Mind:
>These vaults can be moved and changed like any other folder on your system:
>- Just leave the **.obsidian** subfolder and contents alone unless you're confident in what you're changing.
